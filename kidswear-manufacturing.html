Kidswear Production

Children's Clothing Manufactured in Noida

Vansh Enterprises manufactures children's clothing for Indian fashion brands and export buyers. Our kidswear production covers infant, toddler, and older children's ranges, with specific attention to fabric safety, construction durability, and comfort.

All children's garments are produced using soft, tested fabrics. We avoid sharp trims, oversized buttons, and loose cord attachments that present safety hazards for young children. Fabric suitability is checked before sampling begins.

  • Sizes: 0–3M, 3–6M, 6–12M, 1–2Y, 2–3Y, 3–4Y, 4–5Y up to 14 years
  • Soft-tested fabrics: 100% cotton, cotton blends, jersey, fleece
  • Safe construction: no sharp trims, tested button attachment
  • Wash-tested for colour fastness and shrinkage
