About Our Facility

Garment Manufacturing in Noida

Vansh Enterprises operates a dedicated garment manufacturing facility in Noida, Uttar Pradesh — strategically located in Delhi NCR for fast access to India's largest fabric markets, skilled workforce, and national logistics networks.

We manufacture across menswear, womenswear, and kidswear, handling everything from initial consultation and sampling through to bulk production, quality inspection, and packing. Our capacity exceeds 25,000 units per month across multiple product categories.

Whether you're a fashion brand placing your first production order or an established buyer scaling up, our team manages the entire process with clear milestones and proactive communication.

  • Menswear, womenswear, and kidswear across wovens and knits
  • Private label and white-label manufacturing available
  • In-house sampling with 5–7 working day turnaround
  • Bulk production lead time: 4–6 weeks from PO
  • Export documentation for international buyers
Location Advantage

Why Noida for Apparel Manufacturing?

Noida and the Delhi NCR region offer a combination of manufacturing infrastructure, fabric access, and logistics that is hard to match elsewhere in India.

Access to Fabric Markets

Minutes from Gandhi Nagar, Nehru Place, and Chandni Chowk — India's largest wholesale fabric markets for wovens, knits, denims, and specialty fabrics.

Skilled Garment Workforce

Delhi NCR has one of India's deepest pools of trained garment workers — cutters, tailors, finishers, and embellishment specialists.

Pan-India & Export Logistics

Direct connectivity to all major Indian cities and close proximity to Delhi's international airport and inland container depots for export shipments.

FAQ

Questions About Apparel Manufacturing

We manufacture menswear, womenswear, and kidswear across woven and knit fabrics. Product types include shirts, trousers, jackets, kurtas, dresses, tops, co-ord sets, anarkalis, children's wear, and activewear. If you have a specific product in mind, send us your tech pack or reference and we will confirm our capability.

Our standard MOQ is 50 units per style per colour for new buyers. For repeat clients and bulk programs, the same applies. We prefer 200+ units per style for bulk production to ensure cost efficiency. Sampling can be placed for as few as 1–3 pieces per style.

We operate our own manufacturing facility in Noida, Delhi NCR. We do not subcontract production — all garments are made in-house under our direct supervision. This allows us to maintain quality control and accurate lead time commitments.
