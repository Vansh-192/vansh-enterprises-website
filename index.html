How We Work

Our Manufacturing Process

A clear 7-step process ensures every order moves from brief to dispatch without surprises.

Learn About Our Process
1

Consultation & Brief

We discuss your brand requirements, target market, fabrics, and volume. A detailed brief is confirmed before quoting.

2

Sampling & Fit Development

Proto and fit samples in 5–7 days. Revisions until you approve construction, fit, and measurement specs.

3

Fabric & Trim Sourcing

Fabric procured from vetted mills. Swatch approval and GSM/weave testing before bulk purchase.

4

Pattern Making & Cutting

Graded patterns for all sizes. CAD-assisted marker planning for maximum fabric efficiency.

5

Sewing & Assembly

Skilled operators with dedicated lines per category. In-line quality checks at every sewing station.

6

Quality Audit & Inspection

Final audit against buyer-approved tech pack. AQL-based random inspection. Non-conforming units reworked or rejected.

7

Packaging & Dispatch

Brand-compliant packaging, tagging, folding, and carton preparation. Coordinated handoff to courier or freight forwarder.

Quality Control

Built-In Quality at Every Stage

Our QC team doesn't wait for final inspection — quality is checked at five points throughout production. This means problems are caught and corrected before they compound.

  • Fabric inspection on receipt — GSM, shrinkage, colour fastness
  • In-line stitch quality checks at every sewing station
  • Measurement audit against tech-pack specs on every size run
  • AQL final inspection before packing
  • Defect rate consistently below 1%
