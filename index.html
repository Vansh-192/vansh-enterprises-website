Quality & Compliance

Quality isn't an afterthought — it's built into every stage

Our quality systems are aligned to ISO-9001 principles, giving us the discipline and documentation to consistently deliver product that meets your specs — not just most of the time, but every time.

  1. Fabric & Input Inspection

    Every fabric roll and trim batch is inspected on arrival for shade consistency, GSM, width and defect levels before cutting begins.

  2. Inline (In-Process) Checks

    Supervisors carry out stitch count, seam strength and construction checks during the sewing process to catch errors early, not at the end.

  3. End-Line Inspection

    Every completed garment is measured against the approved size chart and visually inspected for finishing, symmetry and presentation before dispatch to finishing.

  4. Final Pre-Shipment AQL Audit

    A final batch audit to AQL 2.5 / 4.0 standards is completed on every order before packing and dispatch. Buyers may also arrange third-party inspection.

  5. Measurement Accuracy & Finishing Controls

    Critical dimensions are logged and tracked throughout production. Pressing, thread trimming and presentation finishing are checked against approved standards.

Sustainability & Workforce

Responsible manufacturing, invested people

We believe that producing well and producing responsibly aren't trade-offs — they're the same goal. Here's how we're building for that.

  • ♻️

    Fabric Waste Reduction

    Marker-efficient cutting and off-cut utilisation programs keep our fabric waste meaningfully below typical industry averages.

  • 🌿

    Eco-Conscious Packaging

    We use recyclable poly bags, reduced plastic packaging and paper-based alternatives wherever operationally viable.

  • 💧

    Water-Aware Operations

    Water consumption within our wet-processing and laundry operations is tracked with a clear reduction roadmap in place.

  • ☀️

    Solar Energy Integration (Roadmap)

    Rooftop solar is part of our near-term facility upgrade plan, reducing grid dependency and our carbon footprint.

  • 📱

    Digital Traceability (Roadmap)

    We are building toward digital production tracking that gives buyers lot-level traceability from fabric origin to finished garment.
