About Vansh Enterprises

A garment manufacturer built for India's new generation of fashion brands — transparent, responsive, and production-focused.

Our Story

Manufacturing Built Around Growing Brands

Vansh Enterprises was founded in Noida with one clear purpose: to give emerging fashion brands, D2C labels, and private-label buyers access to the same quality manufacturing previously available only to large retailers.

Based in Delhi NCR — one of India's most connected garment manufacturing hubs — we operate with direct access to fabric markets, skilled garment workers, and logistics networks that serve both domestic and export buyers.

Our founder Ankush Aggarwal built the business around three principles: honest lead times, consistent quality, and clear communication. Every order is managed by a dedicated team who understands your brand as well as they understand production.

Fair wages, safe working conditions, and ethical sourcing. We manufacture in a way our clients can be proud to talk about.
