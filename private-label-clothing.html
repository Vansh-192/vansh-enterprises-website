What We Offer

Your Brand. Our Manufacturing.

Private label manufacturing means you own the brand — we handle everything behind it. From the initial sample to the final garment tagged with your label and packed in your packaging, every piece leaves our Noida facility representing your brand identity, not ours.

This model works for D2C brands launching their first collection, fashion labels consolidating to one manufacturer, and buyers who want consistent quality and predictable lead times without managing multiple vendors.

We manufacture menswear, womenswear, and kidswear for private label programs, with flexible MOQs starting from 50 units per style.

What's Included

Full Private Label Service

Everything needed to deliver finished, brand-ready garments — from design to dispatch.

Sampling & Development

Proto and fit samples from your tech pack or reference in 5–7 days. We iterate until the garment meets your standard before any bulk is cut.

Fabric & Trim Sourcing

We source fabrics from vetted mills and present options for your approval. We can match to your fabric reference or work from a brief.

Brand Labelling

Woven brand labels, care labels, and size labels sewn into every garment. Label artwork coordination if needed. We work with your approved label supplier or ours.

Quality Control

In-house QC at fabric, inline, and final audit stages. Every unit checked against your approved sample and tech pack specification.

Custom Packaging

Folded and bagged per your specification — polybags, tissue paper, custom boxes, or hangers. Hang tags attached. Ready for your fulfilment centre or retail floor.

Delivery & Dispatch

Pan-India courier delivery or freight handoff. Export documentation for international buyers. We coordinate with your logistics partner or recommend ours.
