Sampling Process

Fast, Accurate Garment Sampling

Our in-house sampling team produces proto and fit samples in 5–7 working days from the date we receive your tech pack, reference garment, or detailed brief. Revision samples follow in 3–4 days.

We recommend 2–3 rounds of sampling to reach a production-approved standard. Each round is reviewed against your tech pack measurements and construction specification before proceeding.

1

Brief & Tech Pack Review

We review your tech pack, reference sample, or brief and confirm any queries before sampling begins.

2

Proto Sample (5–7 days)

First sample produced in your fabric or a similar development fabric to assess construction and silhouette.

3

Fit & Measurement Review

You review the sample and provide fit, construction, and measurement feedback. We confirm corrections.

4

Revision Samples (3–4 days)

Corrections incorporated. Repeat until you approve for pre-production. Typically 2–3 rounds.

5

Pre-Production Sample (PP)

Final production-approved sample in bulk fabric with all trims and labels. Signed off before bulk cutting.

Bulk Production

From PP Approval to Dispatch in 4–6 Weeks

Once your pre-production sample is approved, bulk production follows a structured 6-stage process.

Fabric Procurement

Bulk fabric ordered from approved mill with in-house inspection on delivery — GSM, colour, shrinkage, and width checked.

Pattern Grading & Cutting

Approved patterns graded across all sizes. CAD marker planning for minimal wastage. Cut bundles tracked through production.

Sewing & In-line QC

Dedicated production lines with in-line stitch and measurement checks at every sewing station throughout production.

Final Audit

AQL-based final inspection before packing. Non-conforming units reworked. QC report available on request.

Packing & Tagging

Folded, bagged, hang-tagged per your specification. Cartons packed and labelled with buyer marks and carton contents.

Dispatch

Coordinated handoff to courier, transporter, or freight forwarder. Documents prepared and shipment tracked.
