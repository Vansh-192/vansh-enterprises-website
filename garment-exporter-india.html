Export Buyer Questions

We work on both FOB (Free on Board) and CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight) terms depending on buyer preference. For FOB, we hand off to your nominated freight forwarder. For CIF, we coordinate freight and insurance to the destination port. Discuss your preferred terms when placing your enquiry.

Standard export documentation includes: commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, bill of lading or airway bill, and customs invoice. We can also provide GSP Form A for GSP-eligible countries. Specific certification requirements should be shared at the time of enquiry.
