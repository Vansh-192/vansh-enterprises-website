Menswear Production

Men's Clothing Manufactured in Noida

Vansh Enterprises manufactures a broad range of men's garments for Indian fashion brands and export buyers. Our menswear production covers formal, casual, ethnic, and activewear categories across woven and knit fabrics.

We produce in size ranges XS–4XL with graded patterns, supporting Indian standard sizing, international sizing, or custom size sets for your brand. Fabric sourcing, sampling, and QC are all handled in-house at our Noida facility.
